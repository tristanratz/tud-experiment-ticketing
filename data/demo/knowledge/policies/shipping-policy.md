# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)
- **Free** on orders over $50
- $5.99 on orders under $50

### Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days)
- $12.99 flat rate

### Overnight Shipping (1 business day)
- $24.99 flat rate
- Order must be placed before 2 PM EST

## Processing Time

- Orders typically process within 1-2 business days
- Custom items may take 3-5 business days to process
- During peak seasons, processing may take longer

## Tracking

- Tracking number sent via email once order ships
- Track packages through carrier website or our tracking page
- Updates may take 24 hours to appear after shipping

## International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within the United States and Canada.

**Canada Shipping:**
- 7-14 business days
- Customs fees may apply (customer's responsibility)
- Minimum order $25 USD

## Delivery Issues

### Package Not Received
- Check with neighbors and building management
- Verify delivery address in order confirmation
- Contact us within 48 hours of expected delivery
- We'll open carrier investigation

### Damaged Package
- Inspect package upon delivery
- Take photos of damage
- Contact us within 24 hours
- Keep all packaging materials for investigation
